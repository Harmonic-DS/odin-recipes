Lavash Pizza

Photo by TheDailyGourmet

Description

A thin, flatbread-style pizza. Quick and easy to put together and customize!

Ingredients

  • 1 package lavash flatbread
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 ounces pepperoni, or as needed
  • Additional or alternative toppings per preferance

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Spray one side of lavash flatbread with nonstick spray.
  3. Bake lavash flatbread directly on the oven rack for 2 minutes.
  4. Carefully remove lavash flatbread from oven and place on a baking tray.
  5. Spread each lavash flatbread with 2 to 3 tablespoons of pizza sauce.
  6. Sprinkle 2 ounces of shredded mozzarella cheese onto each lavash.
  7. Top with pepperoni; other toppings can also be added/removed to preferance.
  8. Bake pizza until cheese is fully melted and golden (about 15 minutes).
  9. Carefully remove from oven and let cool for 1 to 2 minutes, then serve.
