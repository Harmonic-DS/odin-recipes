Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie (Strawberry)

Photo by DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS

Description

A delicious yogurt smoothie recipe. You may substitute the strawberries for other berries or fruit!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon of milk

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend until smooth and serve
