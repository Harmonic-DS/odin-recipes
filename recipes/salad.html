Greek Salad

Photo by DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIOS

Description

A delicious salad that's perfect for the summer time! Great paired with romaine and feta.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce-rinsed, dried, and chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 (6 ounce) can of pitted black olives
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 6 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine the chopped/sliced romaine lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, bellpeppers, and red onion in a large bowel
  2. Add the can of pitted black olives and sprinkle with feta cheese
  3. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, and black pepper together in a small bowel
  4. Pour the dressing mix over the salad, toss well to combine and serve
  5. Recipe yields up to 6 servings.
